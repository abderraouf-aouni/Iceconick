Route description

Location

ICECONIC is centrally located at 't Zand - where not only the Saturday market but also all major events take place - and is only 400 meters away from the Grote Markt.

Parking

The underground car park Centrum - 't Zand, accessible via the tunnel under the Boulevard du Roi Albert I, is located a few metres from ICECONIC.

Public transport

From Bruges central station you can easily walk in the direction of 't Zand (10 minutes) or connect directly to bus lines 1, 4, 6, 11, 12, 13, 14 and 16. When leaving the station, the buses, which depart towards the centre in 3 minutes, wait on the left. Get off at the stop 't Zand.
